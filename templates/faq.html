How should I think about sizing when ordering wallpaper?

When ordering our wallpaper, you'll find that we offer one standard size for our rolls.
Each roll is typically 20.5 inches (52 centimeters) wide and 33 feet (10 meters) long,
providing approximately 17 square meters of coverage per roll.

If you need more wallpaper to cover a larger area, you can simply order additional rolls of the same design.
Consider the dimensions of the walls you want to cover and calculate
the total area to determine how many rolls you'll need. Our pattern repeats every 27 inches (68.5 centimeters),
allowing for seamless installation when using multiple rolls.
