Frequently Asked Questions

What types of wallpaper materials do you offer?

We offer a wide range of wallpaper materials including vinyl, non-woven, grasscloth, and paper-based wallpapers. Each material has its own unique characteristics and suitability for different environments. Our product descriptions provide detailed information on the material composition.

Can I see examples of your wallpaper in real homes?

Yes, we showcase customer-submitted photos on our website and social media platforms to provide inspiration and demonstrate how our wallpapers look in real homes. You can also explore our customer reviews for feedback on product quality and installation experiences.

Do you offer installation services?

While we do not offer installation services directly, we provide comprehensive installation guides and resources to help you install your wallpaper with ease. If you require professional installation assistance, we can recommend trusted local contractors in your area.

Can I order a wallpaper sample in a specific color?

Yes, we offer custom color samples for many of our wallpaper designs. Simply contact our customer support team with your color preferences, and we will do our best to accommodate your request. Please note that custom color samples may incur additional fees.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. We also offer alternative payment options such as PayPal and Apple Pay for added convenience. Your payment information is securely processed through our encrypted payment gateway.

How do I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your delivery online. If you have any questions or concerns about your order, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team for assistance.

What is your environmental policy?

We are committed to sustainability and environmental responsibility. Many of our wallpaper products are made from eco-friendly materials and manufactured using environmentally conscious processes. We strive to minimize our carbon footprint and support initiatives that promote conservation and sustainability.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk orders depending on the quantity and total purchase value. Please contact our sales team for more information on bulk pricing and to discuss your specific requirements. We are dedicated to providing competitive pricing and value-added solutions for our customers.

What is your policy on damaged or defective products?

If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will arrange for a replacement or refund as per our warranty policy. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we will do everything we can to resolve any issues promptly and efficiently.

How do I care for and maintain my wallpaper?

Proper care and maintenance are essential for preserving the beauty and longevity of your wallpaper. We recommend gently cleaning your wallpaper with a soft cloth or sponge and mild detergent as needed. Avoid abrasive cleaners or scrubbing, as they may damage the surface. For specific care instructions, refer to the product label or contact our customer support team.

Can I return or exchange my wallpaper if I change my mind?

Yes, we offer a hassle-free return and exchange policy for unused and unopened wallpaper rolls within 30 days of purchase. Simply contact our customer support team to initiate a return or exchange, and we will provide you with instructions for returning the product. Please note that return shipping fees may apply.

Do you offer consultations with design experts?

Yes, we offer personalized consultations with our experienced design experts to help you choose the perfect wallpaper for your space. Whether you need assistance with color selection, pattern matching, or design inspiration, our experts are here to guide you every step of the way. Contact our customer support team to schedule a consultation at your convenience.

How can I stay updated on new product releases and promotions?

Subscribe to our newsletter to receive updates on new product releases, exclusive promotions, and design inspiration straight to your inbox. You can also follow us on social media for the latest news and announcements. Join our community of wallpaper enthusiasts and be the first to know about exciting developments!
